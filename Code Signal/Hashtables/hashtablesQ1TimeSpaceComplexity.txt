hash(key) --> O(1) time and space
put(key, value) --> O(N) time, always O(1) space (no extra space is used)
get(key) --> O(N) time worst case and O(1) average case, O(1) space
delete(key) --> O(N) time, always O(1) space (no extra space is used)
calcLoadFactor() --> O(1) time and space
print() --> O(N) time
